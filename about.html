Explanation of what we did

Happy Valley Subway and Fortress Hill

During the trail along our group project road, we were able to get close to the homeless people, and we were able to form a delicate but strong connection between them along the way. We had the chance to have a conversation with a few homeless people, and we got to catch a glimpse of their experience.

By actually going out and meeting with homeless people and going on kindness walks, we've learnt more about the homeless and more about their perspectives. This helps our final outcome, as we now know more about poverty, and can therefore take action more effectively.

Process

How we helped

  • 01

    Identify

    The first step is to identify the existing problems. We did this by interviewing a homeless person about his difficulties.

  • 02

    Research

    The next step is to research about possible ways to contribute. An example of this is to ask for opinions of homeless people.

  • 03

    Action

    Take action, we will be doing this by adjusting to the needs of homeless people and provide them with the goods they need.

  • 04

    Spread

    We know that it is impossible to make a big difference with such a small group. So the most important step is to spread the word through this website.
