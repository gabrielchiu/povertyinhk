Justin's Reflection

“It matters not the amount of people we help; It matters only the amount of people that see us help”

– A wise person



We started off by going to ‘wellcome’ to buy supplies to donate to the poor(homeless); supplies included a few apples, oranges, and biscuits. We chose to buy things at “wellcome” because they offer large quantities for a reasonable price. We then went to a subway in Happy Valley in which many homeless people stay, we gave out packets of crackers to homeless people.
